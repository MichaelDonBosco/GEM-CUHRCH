🏛️ Glorious Evangelical Ministries (GEM)

Glorious Evangelical Ministries (GEM) is a Pentecostal Christian community dedicated to spreading the message of God’s love through Jesus Christ. The ministry focuses on living a life of love, holiness, and service, reflecting the teachings of the Bible in everyday life.

The church emphasizes that the Gospel is the expression of God’s love, as seen in John 3:16, where God gave His Son for the salvation of the world. GEM is committed not only to preaching the Word but also to demonstrating God’s love through action, especially by caring for the needy, including orphans and widows, as highlighted in James 1:27.

🌍 Mission & Vision
To spread the Gospel to unreached regions
To transform lives through the power of Jesus Christ
To serve communities with compassion and care
To raise and equip leaders for God’s kingdom

The ministry actively engages in mission work across different regions, aiming to bring hope and spiritual growth to people everywhere.

❤️ Core Values
Love – Reflecting God’s unconditional love
Holiness – Living a life set apart for God
Service – Helping those in need
Faith – Trusting God in all circumstances
🙏 What They Do
Conduct worship services and prayer meetings
Share the Gospel through missions
Support communities through outreach programs
Provide spiritual guidance and leadership
